Tips For Exams Success:

  • Create a Study Plan: Break down your study material and allocate specific time slots for each subject. A well-structured plan can help manage your time effectively.
  • Utilize Resources: Take advantage of the resources available to you, including textbooks, lecture notes, and any additional materials provided throughout the semester. Resources needed for students will be published on the website soon.
  • Past Papers: Familiarize yourself with the exam format by practicing past papers. This not only boosts your confidence but also enhances your understanding of question patterns. You can find past exams on several platforms and servers made by students (Google Drive folder and Discord server)
  • Stay Healthy: Adequate sleep, a balanced diet, and regular breaks are crucial for maintaining focus during this intense period. Don't underestimate the power of self-care.
