Privacy

The institute of Electronics and Electrical Engineering IGEE is committed to protecting the privacy and accuracy of confidential information to the extent possible. Other than as required by laws that guarantee public access to certain types of information, or in response to other legal instruments that authorize access, personal information is not actively shared. In particular, we do not re-distribute or sell personal information collected on our web servers.
In addition, and up to this moment, this website operates independently from any official ties with the institute. It is a personal (individual) project and not recognized as the official institute website.

Information Collected:

Our web servers log the following information:

Internet Protocol (IP) address of computer being used.
Web pages requested.
Referring web page.
Browser used.
Date and time.
This website does not associate this data with individual user identities.

Analytics partners:

It's important to note that we do not share any personally identifying information about users with our partners.

We cannot be held responsible for the performance of websites operated by third parties or for any business dealings you may have with them.
